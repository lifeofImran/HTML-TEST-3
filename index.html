We're Always There For You

The word 'Myxa' in ancient Greek means 'lamp nozzle' and Myxa Care's focus is to provide clear and transparent light to guide participants to a brighter future.

Each and every day we aim to support our participants to enlighten their lives and empower them to achieve their goals by providing wide range of services including community participation, developing life skills, shared living skills, group based activities, transportation arrangements, employment and hire education, therapeutic support, and support coordination. Please see additional information about us and the services we provide or Contact Us to book your free consultation.
